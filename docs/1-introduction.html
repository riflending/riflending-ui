Introduction

rLending is a decentralized investment and finance (DEFI) platform that takes advantage on Bitcoin's stability and the RSK ecosystem to allow users to invest in a safe way.

rLenbding-protocol is bases upon our own core Smart Contracts which form an algorithmic distributed protocol deployed on the RSK network. This protocol defines pools of assets with algorithmically derived interest rates, based on the supply and demand for the assets. Suppliers (and borrowers) of an asset interact directly with the protocol, earning (and paying) a floating interest rate, without having to negotiate terms such as maturity, interest rate or collateral with a peer or counterparty.

Our main motivation is to allow users to lend crypto assets as collateral and to borrow them based on interest rates set by real-time supply and demand.

In other words: Lenders earn revenue in exchange for their supplied assets and borrowers pay interests for the assets they used.
